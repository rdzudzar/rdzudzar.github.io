Across Milky Way

I've been approached to write and illustrate astronomy articles for Children's magazine 'Заградка' (Little garden). This adventure was from September 2024 to June 2025. During this time I've created double-spread articles over 10 magazine issues. Illustrations were drawn in Procreate.
