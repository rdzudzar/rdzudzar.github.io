Astronomy outreach:

  • I was co-founder of the 'Novosadska skola astronomije': Introductory astronomy course for general population. The course was supported by the Astronomical Society of Novi Sad, Serbia. Some previous talks (in Serbian) can be found here: Novosadska skola Astronomije
  • As a member of Astronomical Society of Novi Sad I gave plenty of public Astronomy talks and organised observations of the night sky.
